REVIEW

Daniel Alexandre Febuary 10, 2023

Interstellar is definitivly one of the best Sci-fi movies to date. The dystopic future the movie pants encapsulates the audience from the first couple of minutes of the movie. In addtion, the movie maintains a great balence between building the plot and the characters, because of this we as the audience feel as though we are with the crew on their intergalactic space mission and share their emotions.(Its a tearjerker for sure)

One of the most notable parts of the movie include its cinematography, the movie contains some of the most impressive CGI of the twenty-first century, from the black-hole, to other planets, and more this movie will not disaapoint in its production quality. This adds to its realisim and keeps us as viewers glued to the screen even with an above average runtime.

Trivia

  • The black hole depicted in the movie is accurate, and may be the most scientfically accurate 3D model we have so far as it took 100 hours per frame to render the calculations into video.

  • The interviews at the end of the movie in which survivors of the dust bowl share their stories are real, and were taken from interviews in the 1930s.

  • Anne Hathaway who played brand in the film, contracted hypothermia from long shooting sessions in the cold used for the ice planet.
